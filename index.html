Work

Brigham Health Hub

Brigham affectionately known as my “baby” was the first project I was thrust on to since working in Hill Holliday. Brigham, a locally recognizable and loved hospital, was transforming into a national brand – Brigham Health. Set to compete with brands such as Mayo Clinic. A large departure from the highly technical aspect of what I’d previously worked on, it was a perfect mix of consumer centricity and trending tech capabilities. What started off as a content strategy, morphed into a personalization and test and learn strategy encompassing site, email, and social development. And the results paid off, with an increase in Avg Page views, TOS, Email opens and CTR, they’re well on their way to a national threat.
